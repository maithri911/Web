SMVITM Achieves Autonomous Status

Sri Madhwa Vadiraja Institute of Technology and Management (SMVITM), Bantakal, has officially been granted autonomous status, marking a significant milestone in its academic journey. This recognition empowers the institution to design its own curriculum, conduct examinations, and foster innovation in teaching and learning.

New Student Committee Formed

In a move to enhance student engagement and leadership, SMVITM has constituted a new Student Committee comprising representatives from all departments. The committee will play a vital role in organizing events, addressing student concerns, and promoting campus culture.

Orientation Program Welcomes First-Year Students

On Sunday, SMVITM hosted a formal orientation program for the incoming batch of first-year students. The event included an introduction to campus facilities, academic expectations, and motivational talks by faculty and alumni. The Principal emphasized the importance of discipline, innovation, and holistic development.
